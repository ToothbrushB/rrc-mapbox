Breck homes that have RRCs on them

The homes on this map are homes where the directory address matches an address in the Mapping Prejudice database.

Although not represented on the map, faces/names were manually processed to get a better idea of the racial demographics of this group. This process is very subjective, but we feel that it resulted in a more nuanced view that took mixed race people into account. In the end, we found that the group was about 80% white.

Each pin on the map represents an address. Click on the pin to get a list of the students who are associated with the address, their grades, and their estimated race/gender.

Disclaimer: Race/gender data was estimated by the FairFace machine learning model using Veracross photos. As a result, there are errors and gaps in this aspect of the data. Self-reported information would be much more accurate.
