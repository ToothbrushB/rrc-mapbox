Breck homes that have RRCs on them

The 50 homes on this map are homes where the directory address matches an address in the Mapping Prejudice database.

Although not represented on the map, faces/names were manually processed to get a better idea of the racial demographics of this group. This process is very subjective (and inherently biased), but I feel that it resulted in a more accurate and nuanced view that took mixed race people into account. In the end, we found that the group was about 80% white (see plot below).

Each pin on the map represents an address. Click on the pin view the covenant text/year as well as a list of the students who are associated with the address, their grades, and their estimated race/gender. If an address has multiple covenants associated with it, only one of them is shown.

Disclaimer: Race/gender data was estimated by the FairFace machine learning model using Veracross photos. As a result, there are errors and gaps in this aspect of the data. Self-reported information would be much more accurate.
